How to Play

Green balls are your soldiers.

Red balls are enemies.

Each enemy killed gives +3 gold.

You can buy 1 soldier for 5 gold.

To buy, just tap on the table!

Every round, 2 more enemies arrive.

But remember...

If you recruit too many soldiers,

they feel unimportant and lose morale.

Too many soldiers may stop following orders.

Think before you buy. Power is balance.
